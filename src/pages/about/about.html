Description

The safekeeping app is a personal security app that allows you to save and share your location as well as upload media files into the cloud.
Going out on a date with the new man?
Have a blind date?
Launch SafeKeeping before your date and enjoy the comfort that your location and any files you upload go into the cloud should any problems arise.
Going for a hike? Use the SafeKeeping app to keep track of your whereabouts regardless of whether or not you have an Internet connection. Share your location with friends, family and loved ones or leave it on private mode to secure your location with a trackable breadcrumb that the authorities can follow.
